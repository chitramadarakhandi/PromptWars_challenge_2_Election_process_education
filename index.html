Step-by-Step Election Guide

1. Voter Registration

Ensure you are registered to vote before the deadline. You can usually register online or in person.

2. Verification

Check your local requirements for voter ID. Keep necessary documents ready for polling day.

3. Voting Day

Visit your designated polling booth. Cast your vote securely and confidentially.

4. Counting & Results

Votes are counted securely, and results are declared by election authorities transparently.
